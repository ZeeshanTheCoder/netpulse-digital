What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is a smart way to promote your business online. It includes using platforms like Google, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, emails, and websites to reach the right audience at the right time.

Why is it Important?

In today’s digital world, your customers are online—your business should be too. Digital marketing helps you reach the right audience, at the right time, on platforms like Google, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, email, and your website. Whether you want to boost brand awareness, drive traffic, or get quality leads, digital marketing offers measurable and cost-effective ways to grow.
